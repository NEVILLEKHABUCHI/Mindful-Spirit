Who We Are

We specialize in teaching a variety of healing techniques designed to help individuals navigate and overcome a range of mental health issues and emotional challenges. Our comprehensive approach addresses common concerns such as stress, anxiety, trauma and depression, which often arise as a result of these issues. Additionally, we focus on promoting overall well-being and resilience, enabling our clients to effectively manage any illnesses that may stem from these emotional and mental struggles. Our goal is to empower individuals by equipping them with the skills and tools necessary for lifelong healing and personal growth.

The Convergence of Healing, Psychotherapy, and Spiritual Practice



More and more, the role of spirituality in healing is being recognized and integrated in various fields, including psychotherapy, addiction recovery, and medicine. Inside the program, we will look at how our spiritual journey can influence our path of transformation and be a powerful resource in working with trauma and wounding.

When we include the spiritual dimension in healing work, we are able to access information from the soul level as well as from our body and our ancestral lineage. It is this unique combination that pens new insights and can accelerate our growth and healing.

Of course, it is important to guard against spiritual bypassing, both on our own path and when we work with clients.

Whether we follow a particular religion or tradition, or whether our spiritual path is more self-directed, with proper guidance and care we can draw upon deeper spiritual wisdom and insights when navigating the difficult terrain of personal, ancestral and collective traumas.
